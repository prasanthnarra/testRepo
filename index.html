Prasanth weds Shruthi
9th March 2014
This moment is perfect, to tell you a story,
Of two people most ordinary,
who will soon come together to start a new era of glory.
Please bear with us if our lines don’t rhyme,
we’re putting together something like this for the first time.
So, let’s see... Before we were first told of each other,
Our lives were sufficiently busy to not really bother;
of what was in store for us.
First week of October 2013 is what we rightly remember,
The time that brought along a change to be cherished forever.

When I was first told of Sruthi, my heart felt that “She was the One”;
October 13 only confirmed that my season for love had just begun.
It was a bliss, when I heard her say,
“I always knew you were the one, and had waited for you many a night and day...”

Until now, these lines meant nothing but typical “Bollywood” brew,
But suddenly their intervention seemd realistic too...
And then the story went on, as a new life of love and care was bestowed upon Us,
where my ambition became to fulfil all her dreams, & she discovered my love for musical themes.
Slowly, ice creams replaced laddus, & fondness grew for sarees, All we hoped for was, “May it pass sooner! this month of February”...
And now that Feb is almost past and March is rushing, We invite all of you for your love and blessings.

Please do come, and grace the occasion, of our union this beautiful season.
